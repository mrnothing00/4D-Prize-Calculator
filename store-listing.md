## Full Description (Google Play / App Store)

MY 4D Calculator is a free, offline prize calculator for Malaysian 4D lottery players. Instantly see how much you can win for any 4-digit number across all bet types — Straight, iBox, and Box.

Whether you play Magnum, Toto, Damacai, CashSweep, Sabah 88, Sandakan, or any other Malaysian 4D operator, the prize structure is the same and this app covers them all.

KEY FEATURES

- Calculate prizes for Straight, iBox, and Box bets
- Set Big (ABC) and Small (A) amounts independently
- See complete prize breakdowns for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, Special, and Consolation prizes
- Combined totals when you bet both Big and Small
- Live ticket cost preview before you calculate
- Automatic permutation detection (24, 12, 6, 4, or 1 permutations)
- Calculation history — quickly recall and reload past calculations
- Clean, easy-to-read interface designed for all ages
- Works completely offline — no internet required
- No ads, no tracking, no data collection
- Supports English and Chinese (中文)

HOW IT WORKS

1. Enter your 4-digit number
2. Choose your bet type: Straight, iBox, or Box
3. Set your Big and/or Small bet amounts
4. Tap "Calculate Winnings" to see your full prize breakdown

PRIZE REFERENCE (per RM1 bet)

Straight:
- 1st Prize: Big RM2,500 / Small RM3,500
- 2nd Prize: Big RM1,000 / Small RM2,000
- 3rd Prize: Big RM500 / Small RM1,000
- Special: Big RM180
- Consolation: Big RM60

iBox prizes are automatically adjusted based on permutation count. Box bets cover all permutations at full Straight prize value.

ABOUT

MY 4D Calculator is a utility tool for informational and educational purposes only. It helps you understand potential prize amounts before placing a bet. This app does not facilitate gambling, sell lottery tickets, or connect to any lottery operator.

Built with privacy in mind — all calculations happen on your device. No data is collected, stored on servers, or shared with third parties.
